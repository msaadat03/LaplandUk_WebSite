Getting Here

Travelling to LaplandUK

LaplandUK is situated at Whitmoor Forest, Swinley Road, Ascot, Berkshire, SL5 8BD and is approximately 1.5miles from Ascot Station.

There is free onsite car parking at Lapland



Trains

The closest station to Whitmoor Forest is Ascot. This station provides easy access to the event from London and Reading.

To find out the quickest journey time by train and the best fare, visit mytrainticket



Buses

The 162 or 162A bus leave approximately every hour from Ascot High Street and stops a short walk from the Lapland UK site. For more details regarding Routes, please click here, for Timetables, click here.

Taxis

The local taxi company Ascot Cars operate from directly outside Ascot Station

They can be contacted on:

01344 297297



Cycles

Lapland UK provide dry bike parking onsite.
