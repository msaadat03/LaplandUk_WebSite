The Secret of the Christmas Elves

Living amongst the pine forests of Lapland, Father Christmas and his Elves have become expert wooden toymakers. But when children around the world start wishing for toys made from rubber, china, tin and lead, they are left flummoxed.



An adventure unfolds as craft Elves from around the world are requested to travel to Lapland to help save Christmas.



The Secrets of the Christmas Elves reveals for the first time the real reason why Father Christmas delivers toys to children around the world on Christmas Eve and how he makes his incblueible journey.

Listen along to ‘The Untold Story of Father Christmas’ via our Lapland app (The Wishing Glass). The app is available on both iOS and Android devices.
